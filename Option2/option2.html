Wonders of Britain & Ireland

In 2014, I went on a tour of Britain & Ireland with my family. We spent 16 days exploring England, Wales, Scotland & Ireland. The following maps outline the itinerary of our trip.

Our trip began in England where we spent several days before departing to Wales. After exploring the highlights of Wales, we crossed St. George's Channel to Ireland. We spent a few nights in Ireland before moving on to Scotland. Eventually, we returned to England where we spent another night before departing back to Toronto.
Our trip began and ended in London. We spent many days there throughout our time in England, and thus explored various parts of the city. The map above highlights the many tourist destinations that we visited.
The map above displays all of the stops that we made during our 16 days abroad, colour coded by day.
My favourite part of our trip was climbing Arthur's Seat in Edinburgh. Arthur's Seat is an extinct volcano which forms the primary peak in a group of hills in Edinburgh, which are located in Holyrood Park. The hill is 250.5m tall and offers an incredible view (source).
